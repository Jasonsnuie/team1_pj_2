About Us

The One and Only GLOBAL Medical Service Finder

It's not only about whether you can find a hospital or not. You have to find it, you have to find your way to the hospital. Even then, it might be closed. Even if it is open, you will have very hard time communicating with the medical staff.

We will help you find it, get to it, and walk you through the entire oredeal while at the hospital.

Our vision is to make the world a safer place for travelers from all around the globe. Being sick or getting hurt in your own country is bad, but you know how to deal with it. However, if you are traveling or in a foreign land, the situtation goes from bad to disastrous very quickly. Short of calling 911, you don't know what to do, or where to go. More than likely, this will be more than enough to ruin at least a couple of days of your trip. Our goal is to eliminate this problem by allowing travelers to find, receive immiedate and appropriate medical service wherever you are around the world.
